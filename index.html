About Me


Hi there, my name is Vanita (Pronounced vuh-knee-taah). I graduated from the University of Washington (GO HUSKIES!!!) with a Bachelor's in Biochemistry and minored in Anthropology and Hindi.


I have worked in Finance, Accounting, and the Healthcare/Insurance industry for over half a decade. I aspire to be a full-stack develeoper and am currently enrolled in a Coding Bootcamp at the University of Washington.


I constantly look for new challenges, experiment endlessly and am always learning new things. In my free time I love to choreograph/dance to a mixture South Asian music, go hiking, do water activities and try new things. Check out my portfolio to find out more about me :)
